Key highlights

  • Growth campaigns wait 2.5× longer for approval Growth team submissions spend an average of 33 days pending, compared to just 13 days for Brand Partnerships — the fastest-moving team.
  • April saw a major spike in submissions Submissions jumped to 93 in April (vs 36 in March), with 66 of those coming from the Growth team alone. May remained elevated at 73.
  • Brand Partnerships leads on approval rate at 91% Growth sits at 70% and Brand Social at 33%, suggesting Brand Social content faces the most revision cycles before approval.
  • 71 submissions still pending approval (27% of YTD) Out of 259 total year-to-date submissions, more than a quarter remain in queue waiting on bank sign-off.
  • Growth dominates volume but faces the most friction Growth accounts for 58% of all YTD submissions yet consistently experiences longer wait times and lower approval rates than Brand teams.
  • Longest-pending campaign: "Savings APY Benefit Trigger" at 113 days Submitted by Growth on February 10, 2026, this campaign has spent over 3 months in Sutton revision cycles with no final approval as of June 3.
